--- layout: page root: true title: tagline: rOpenGov version: 0.3 ghurl: https://github.com/louhos/louhos.github.com language: en group: project translation: index.html --- {% include JB/setup %} Algorithms for Open Government Data and Computational Social Science

We have recently seen an avalanche of open data on elections, economics, demography, traffic, and other fields relevant to computational social science. Analytical tools are needed to take full advantage of these new information resources, including tools to access, analyse and communicate such information in a fully transparent and reproducible manner.

A versatile, community-driven ecosystem of analytical software packages dedicated to social and political sciences can provide a solution. This could revolutionize the field as has already happened for biological sciences during the last decade, and build on lessons learned from analogous projects such as Bioconductor.

We are now building a community of independent developers around open goverment data analytics. We are calling for contributions in the form of R packages. By joining the community you will get added visibility and recognition for your project and an opportunity to share ideas, attract contributors, and pool the efforts with others working on related topics. If you are writing related software on other languages than R, let us know. Don't hesitate to contact us!

Projects:

  • Austria Grazwahl2012 R tools for Austria elections
  • Finland sorvi R package for open government data in Finland
  • Russia R tools for open data in Russia - coming soon
  • Further projects coming, submissions welcome!
